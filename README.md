# Privacy Policy

**Last updated:** 12/06/2026

This Privacy Policy explains how **Do One Thing** ("the App") handles your personal data.

Do One Thing is operated by **Camille Le Lan-Toson**, located in France.

For any privacy-related question, you can contact us at:

**clelantoson.pro@gmail.com**

## 1. Overview

Do One Thing is an iOS task management app designed to help you focus on one task at a time.

The App is designed to be **local-first**. It does not require an account, login, or a personal backend server operated by us.

Most of your data stays on your device or within Apple services such as Apple Reminders and iCloud, depending on your own Apple settings.

## 2. Data Stored Locally or Through Apple Services

### Tasks and Reminders

Do One Thing uses Apple Reminders through EventKit to read, create, update, and manage tasks.

Your tasks are stored by Apple Reminders and may be synced through iCloud depending on your Apple account settings. We do not operate a server that stores your tasks.

### Local Preferences

Some App preferences are stored locally on your device, including settings related to the App experience. These preferences are stored using local device storage.

### Local Notifications

The App may schedule local notifications to remind you of your tasks.

These notifications are generated locally on your device. We do not use server-side push notifications.

### Widget Background Image

The App may request access to your photo library only when you manually choose a background image for the widget.

The selected image is resized locally and stored in the App's shared container (App Group) so that the widget can display it.

No image is transmitted to a server. The App does not keep continuous access to your photo library between selections. It does not scan, index, or re-read your photo library.

On iOS 14 and later, the App supports limited photo library access.

## 3. Permissions Requested by the App

The App may request the following iOS permissions:

### Reminders

Used to read and write tasks through Apple Reminders.

### Notifications

Used to schedule local reminders on your device.

### Photo Library

Used only when you manually choose a widget background image.

The photo library access is read-only and used only for the selected image.

## 4. Third-Party Services

The App may use third-party services to operate, improve, and maintain the App.

### PostHog

PostHog may be used for analytics, such as understanding how the App is used and improving the user experience.

Analytics data may include technical information such as device type, App version, operating system version, usage events, and general interaction data.

We do not intentionally send your task content to PostHog.

### Sentry

Sentry may be used for crash reporting and error monitoring.

Crash reports may include technical information such as device type, App version, operating system version, stack traces, and diagnostic information needed to understand and fix bugs.

We do not intentionally send your task content to Sentry.

### RevenueCat

RevenueCat is used to manage subscriptions and in-app purchases.

RevenueCat may process information related to your subscription status, purchase history, App Store transaction identifiers, and related technical data.

### Apple App Store

Payments and subscriptions are processed by Apple through the App Store.

We do not process your payment card details. Apple handles payment processing according to its own terms and privacy policy.

## 5. Data We Do Not Collect

Do One Thing does not require you to create an account.

We do not collect:

- your name;
- your email address, unless you contact us directly;
- your payment card details;
- your task content on our own servers;
- your widget images on our own servers;
- precise location data;
- contacts;
- advertising identifiers for targeted advertising.

## 6. Legal Basis for Processing

Where the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR) applies, we rely on the following legal bases:

- **Performance of a contract**, when processing is necessary to provide the App and its features.
- **Legitimate interests**, when using analytics, crash reporting, fraud prevention, security, or service improvement tools.
- **Consent**, when iOS asks you to grant access to permissions such as Reminders, Notifications, or Photos.

## 7. Data Retention

Data stored locally on your device remains there until you delete it, change your settings, remove related Apple Reminders, or uninstall the App.

Data processed by third-party services such as PostHog, Sentry, RevenueCat, or Apple may be retained according to their own retention policies.

If you contact us by email, we may keep your message for as long as necessary to respond to your request and maintain appropriate records.
